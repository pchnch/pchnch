MISSION
To optimize, source, and supply built-in multifunctional convection systems and sanitary equipment across the USA, CIS countries, and the UAE.
In just five years, we have grown into a modern, multi-million-dollar business with steady expansion and a strong proven reputation. Our next goal is to firmly establish Global Convector in the U.S. market — making it our primary operating base.
We see tremendous emerging opportunities across the American construction desktop and are committed to expanding partnerships and delivering exceptional value to our clients.
HISTORY
Global Convector began its journey in 2021 in Dubai, driven by the idea of delivering sustainable, reliable construction materials and providing integrated support for development projects. Over the past five years, we have successfully supplied more than 20 large-scale construction projects.
Our strongest area of expertise has become built-in multifunctional convection systems and premium sanitary equipment. Due to consistently high demand from the U.S. construction sector, we made a strategic decision to expand our operations and focus on growth in the United States.
Today, our company works with dozens of trusted partners across the USA, UAE, and CIS countries, supported by well-established logistics from exclusive manufacturers in Europe and China.
EXCLUSIVE PROVIDER OF CONSTRUCTION EQUIPMENT
Global Convector was founded in 2021 in Dubai (ext. Construction Trading in UAE) with a clear mission: to bring durable, high-quality construction materials to the market, support major building projects, and build long-term partnerships with developers and manufacturers.
